ABOUT US

Buzz has been in business for 20 years. From the beginning our company sought to serve clients by being innovative and brining the best apparel to our clients. We have achieved that by working to create a network of suppliers providing us with the best materials to enable Buzz to gain a reputation for care and quality in the industry that is second to none.

We are committed to continuing to work hard and further invest to become the apparel company in the world and create value for retailers, improve performance and excite consumers. This is the way we at Buzz can achieve the returns on investment that you, as our shareholder, deserve. In January, our majority shareholder we announced plans to distribute parts of its Buzz shares to shareholders (dividend in kind).

Our goal is to build on our successes each year as we look to grow our efficiencies, partnerships, and products to benefit our clients and investors.
